Our Story
Newark, The Jets, and an Ornament...

Ben and Siobhan met while they were both working for the Board of Education in Newark. However, it was not until Siobhan left Newark for a new job that Ben decided to ask her out. On their first date, they went to watch a Jets game at a local bar. Even though the Jets lost, Siobhan and Ben left as winners because that was the last first date they would ever have. Ben completely surprised Siobhan last Christmas when he came home with a very special Ornament asking her to marry him. Ben and Siobhan can't wait to share their love with all their friends and family on August 8, 2020!
